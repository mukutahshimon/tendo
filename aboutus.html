Agape Love (Unconditional Love)

Agape love is the unconditional love God has for us. (John 3:16, 1 John 4:8)

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." - John 3:16

Phileo Love (Brotherly Love)

Phileo love is the affection and friendship between believers. (Romans 12:10, 1 Thessalonians 4:9)

"Be devoted to one another in love. Honor one another above yourselves." - Romans 12:10

Eros Love (Romantic Love)

Eros love is the romantic love between husband and wife. (Ephesians 5:25-33, Song of Solomon)

"Husbands, love your wives, just as Christ loved the church and gave himself up for her." - Ephesians 5:25
